Pow! Wow! Hawaii 2017 Schedule

Thursday

February 9th 7:30

STUDY HALL WITH THE POW! WOW! TEAM

Talk and press event with the Directors of POW! WOW! Located at the Modern Honolulu, 1775 Ala Moana Boulevard.

Saturday

February 11th 6pm - 10pm

OPENING BLOCK PARTY PRESENTED BY POW! WOW! AND THE HONOLULU NIGHT MARKET

Block party in Kaka'ako to celebrate the start of POW! WOW! Hawaii 2017. Music by DJ Cipha Sounds. Located on Cooke Street.

Saturday

February 11th 10pm - 2am

AFTER PARTY PRESENTED BY POW! WOW! AND MONSTER ENERGY

After party for the opening block party. Music by DJ Cipha Sounds. Located at Artisty on 461 Cooke Street.

Sunday

February 12th 5pm - 10am

THE 4TH ANNUAL ‘POW! WOW! EXPLORING THE NEW CONTEMPORARY MOVEMENT’

Curated by Andrew Hosner from Thinkspace Gallery. Located at the Honolulu Museum of Art School, 1111 Victoria Street. More Information

Monday

February 13th all day

MURAL PAINTING BEGINS

Artists paint murals and create installation all over Kaka'ako. Painting from February 13th to the 18th. See map for locations.

Tuesday

February 14th 6pm - 10pm

5TH ANNUAL 1XRUN X POW! WOW! PRINT EXHIBITION

Limited edition print exhibition presented by 1xRun. Located at Lana Lane Studios, 327 Lana Lane.

Wednesday

February 15th 7pm - 8pm

JEFF HAMADA OF BOOOOOOOM TALKS TO MR. JAGO

Jeff Hamada of Booooooom has an in-depth conversation with acclaimed artist, Mr. Jago. Held at the Kaka'ako Agora, 441 Cooke Street.

Thursday

February 16th 7pm - 9pm

SAVING BANKSY” PREMIERE AND Q&A.

Premiere of the "Saving Banksy" documentary and a Q&A with the creators of the film. Located at Kaka’ako Agora, 441 Cooke Street. Hosted by OluKai.

Friday

February 17th 6pm - late

ART TO THE PITCH

“Art to the Pitch” an art inspired soccer match by Kicks to the Pitch, Paradise Soccer Club and participating POW! WOW! artists. Located at Wade Warehouse, 449 Cooke Street.

Friday

February 17th 6pm - 11pm

SHADOW STYLES ANNUAL BREAKIN EVENT

A break dance battle with the UDEF crew. Located at the Kaka’ako Agora, 441 Cooke Street.

Saturday

February 18th 4pm - 9pm

HAWAIIAN MUSIC SHOWCASE

A showcase of some of the greatest Hawaiian musicians. Located at 1011 Ala Moana Boulevard.

Saturday

February 18th 5pm - 11pm

ELECTRIC PALMS, DIM MAK, AND POW! WOW! PRESENTS THE DIM MAK 20TH ANNIVERSARY TOUR WITH STEVE AOKI

Electric Palms, DIM MAK, and POW! WOW! presents the DIM MAK 20th Anniversary Tour with Steve Aoki, ILoveMakonnen, Autoerotique, Max Styler, and Special Guests. Get your tickets here.

Saturday

February 18th 11pm - 2am

POW! WOW! AFTER PARTY

POW! WOW! after party at the Addiction in the Modern Honolulu. Located at 1775 Ala Moana Blvd.
