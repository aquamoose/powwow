Colasa

Colasa studied fine arts and is unwilling to conform to the a formal system of art and instead turns to expressing his artistic qualities through rebellious tactics. His focus is mixing different media and performance techniques, and he continues to introduce new ideas. With a unique style of visual representation and integration of oriental ink painting style, he has established a high reputation in the international community. He is a well-seasoned and has participated in many global art exhibitions and commercial projects with well-known brands such as NIXON, Adidas, and Agnès b Jieyou.
